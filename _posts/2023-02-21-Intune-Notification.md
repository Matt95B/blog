## Enrolment notification

### Email notification
```html
<p><strong>Device Enrolment Confirmation</strong></p>

<p>Your device has been successfully enrolled in <strong>Microsoft Intune</strong>.</p>

<p>If you did <strong>NOT</strong> enrol this device, please contact your Cyber Security team (cybersecurity@yourdomain.com) immediately.</p>
```

## Compliance notification

### Email notification
```html
<p>Our records indicate that your Windows device has been flagged as non-compliant.</p>
<br>
<p>To restore compliance, please:</p>
<p>	1.	Open the Company Portal app on your device.</p>
<p>	2.	Review the compliance issues listed.</p>
<p>	3.	Rectify the flagged anomaly.</p>
<br>
<p>Until your device is compliant, access to sensitive applications and data will be restricted.</p>
<p>Thank you for taking prompt action to help keep our systems secure.</p>
```
